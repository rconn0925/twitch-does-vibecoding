## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Real Twitch platform → live process | First time real network events drive the system (checkpoint only) |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-23 | Repudiation / Denial of Service | silent vote loss across crash or reconnect | mitigate | recovery.e2e.test.ts proves acknowledged votes survive restart and reconciliation re-syncs memory from the ledger; the round_closed audit row records the final tally for after-the-fact review |
| T-02-24 | Denial of Service | votes sent during a genuine connectivity gap | accept | EventSub has no event replay (verified from source, RESEARCH.md Pattern 1) — no consumer could recover them; documented honestly in OPERATIONS.md and in the console's "Twitch connection lost" copy so the streamer can re-run a compromised round manually |
| T-02-25 | Spoofing | live checkpoint performed against a wrong/test app identity | mitigate | Checkpoint steps verify the authorized account IS the broadcaster (scopes bind to the logged-in user); OPERATIONS.md warns to log into the broadcaster account before /auth/start |
</threat_model>
