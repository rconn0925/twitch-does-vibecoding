<objective>
Close the loop on graceful failure (BUILD-03): build failures and mid-build model refusals are narrated on stream and in chat with retry/skip — never silent dead air. Refusals are first-class narrated events (D3-08), not errors. Transient/tooling errors auto-retry at most once (D3-09); content/compliance failures never auto-retry. The streamer picks retry or skip from the operator console (new routes mirroring the veto route). This plan also lands the final integration proof: the full end-to-end failure and veto-abort e2e (BUILD-04's in-flight abort exercised against a running sandboxed build with fakes), and the console build-awareness + veto-abort confirmation copy.

Purpose: A live show fails in public — every failure produces a narrated, actionable moment on the console and in chat, and the veto always wins cleanly.
Output: extended `build-session.ts`, `narration.ts`, `operator-console/server.ts` + console assets, `main.ts` wiring, and `tests/e2e/build-failure.e2e.test.ts`.
</objective>
