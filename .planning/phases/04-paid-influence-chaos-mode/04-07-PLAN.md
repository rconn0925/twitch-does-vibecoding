| T-04-23 | Elevation of Privilege | !build routing around the gate | mitigate | !build during a window routes only through controlWindow.submitInstruction -> submitDuringWindow -> classify(); no direct enqueue (single-funnel invariant enforces) |

| T-04-25 | Denial of Service | Donation socket drop crashing the app mid-stream | mitigate | connectStreamElements treats disconnect as transient (log+reconnect); donations degrade to reconnecting/unconfigured, voting+builds unaffected (never-silent console box) |
