Bank a PENDING free-reign window when a donation/redemption arrives while the machine is busy (BUILD_IN_PROGRESS / VOTING_ROUND / CHAOS_MODE), instead of denying. Purpose: real money mid-build gets honored, not refused — without touching the compliance gate, the paid funnel, the duration mapping, or the paid/chaos separation.
Output: extended ControlWindow FSM + persistence (pending status), new audit kind `window_pending`, new narration beats, pending-aware console panel/Revoke, scheduler-race proof, all under the never-silent doctrine.
